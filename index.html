~ FAQs ~

How do I order?
Email – plattersandboards@hotmail.com with the following: Information about your event - Date of your event - What time range you expect food to be ready - How many guests you are expecting - Which menu & add ons you will like and any adjustments -
How long does it take to set up a table?
Depending on the size of the grazing table, it can take up to 2.5 hours.
How long does the food last?
On average, food should be consumed within a 4-hour period. However, temperatures exceeding 8 degrees can decrease this period to up to 2 hours. If temperatures around the grazing table can be made cooler, this will be advised to help your grazing table last longer!
What areas do you cater for?
We are primarily based in Milton Keynes but cover surrounding areas such as; Bedford, Luton and Buckingham. Any event located over 10 miles can be arranged but will be at an extra cost. Get in touch for more information, we will be happy to accommodate.
How much notice do I need to give before making a booking?
We accept a minimum of 1 weeks' notice for any package. The weeks' notice will include the deposit being paid, any later, your booking is not confirmed. Please give as much notice as possible, this will allow Platters & Boards to work with you to create the perfect set up and personalised menu for your day.
Do you clear up after an event?
Yes, we do
Do you have a vegan menu or cater for allergens?
We do not specifically have a vegan or allergens menu but can cater to alternatives. Please contact us and we can arrange this together.
Are Platters and Boards insured?
Yes, we are fully insured. Please contact us for more information if needed.
Does Platters and Boards have any Hygiene Certifications?
Yes, we are certified in Food Safety and Hygiene for Catering. We can present our certificate upon request.
Do you need a kitchen to set up?
No, we do not. We are able to ensure the food is kept fresh and safe before serving with equipment suitable to do so. We are able to set up the grazing table absolutely anywhere.
When do you expect final payment?
Final payment should be paid no later than 48 hours before the event. Failing to do so can affect whether your booking will be continued and your deposit being returned. This is due to other events that could have been booked on the day.
How much deposit needs to be paid and when will this be returned?
We require a £100 deposit which will be returned within 3 days after the event. This is to ensure the tools and props used to display the foods are carefully checked afterwards away from the venue.
